How to Play

How to play:

1. Select a restriction enzyme from the sidebar or dropdown.

2. Click Cut Plasmid to cut pUC19 at that enzyme's recognition site(s).

3. Click Show All Sites to display all MCS enzyme recognition sites on the map.

4. Click on a feature or restriction site in the circular map for details.

5. Use the Analysis tab to see overhang details and ligation predictions.

Scoring: +10 points for cuts that are directional (different 5' and 3' ends), +5 for any valid cut. Bonus for consecutive cuts without resetting.
